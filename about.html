Founded in 2022 by Albert Ashaba Aheebwa, The AAA Blog is your how-not-to guide for developing software by self-taught developers. We rehash tales of cataclysmic development, from project management gone fabulously wrong to incomprehensible coding choices.

This blog is for those starting out and are trying to hack their way into this field with too much to learn and too many resources to choose from in so little time.

This is not a school, but rather a hitchhiker's guide on what not-to-do if you wish not to fail at software development before you even start.

Don't Panic!

NOTE: You might have noticed that code examples and tutorials in this blog are weiredly most - if not all - written in the python programming language. This is because your lovely author, even though he's multilingual, prefers the easiest and best programming language. #EverybodyLovesPython
